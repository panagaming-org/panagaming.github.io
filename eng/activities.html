Our Activities

At Pana Gaming, we encourage participation, learning, and fun, creating unique experiences for all our members.

Minecraft Servers

Interactive and creative spaces where members can build, explore, and embark on adventures together, fostering collaboration, creativity, and fun in a safe environment.

Game Meetups

We organize gaming sessions for titles like Repo and other popular games, offering a meeting point for fun and camaraderie among all members.

Training and Workshops

Cybersecurity and online safety: teaching how to browse safely and protect personal information. Neurodiversity awareness: upcoming workshops to better understand neurodivergent individuals and promote inclusion.

Community Meetings

Virtual and in-person gatherings to share ideas, experiences, and personal projects. A key space to strengthen bonds, listen to all voices, and grow together as a community.
