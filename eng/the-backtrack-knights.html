The Backtrack Knights

The Backtrack Knights is the cybersecurity and development team at Pana Gaming: a team where discipline, cunning, and technical skills come together to protect our networks, servers, and projects.

We operate in the shadows, analyze threats, track anomalies, and restore digital peace to the realm. We are the knights of backtracking: learning from the past, mastering the present, and safeguarding the future.

Our Mission

At The Backtrack Knights, our mission is to safeguard the integrity and security of Pana Gaming's digital systems. We are dedicated to identifying and neutralizing cyber threats, protecting sensitive data, and ensuring a safe environment for our users and projects. Through constant innovation and continuous learning, we aim to anticipate future challenges, ensuring that Pana Gaming remains a bastion of digital security.

Inspiration

Our group is based on creating a hacker community that embraces the technological culture that flourished between the 1980s and 1990s.

Our inspiration comes directly from the original sources of hacker culture, including: The Hacker Manifesto by "The Mentor" and The Four Freedoms of Free Software (Richard Stallman).

The genesis of The Backtrack Knights traces back to the creation of ARPANET, when the expansion and development of modern computers gained momentum. Initially, the use of computers and networks was strictly restricted to business and educational environments. The key inspiration for this project lies in reflecting on the ethical and philosophical questions that arose from this era of exclusive technology, exploring the limits and impact of initially elitist digital knowledge.

What We Do

The Backtrack Knights are dedicated to protecting and securing Pana Gaming's digital systems and those of our partners. We teach best practices, perform security audits, and develop customized tools to strengthen our defenses.

Beyond cybersecurity, we actively participate in software development, creating tools, applications, and solutions that strengthen Pana Gaming's mission. For us, technology is not a product to sell, but a means to build freedom, collaboration, and mutual support.

We strongly believe in free development and shared knowledge. That‚Äôs why, at Pana Gaming and The Backtrack Knights, we prioritize free and open-source software (FOSS) over closed platforms. We invest in technologies that do not restrict, control, or overshadow users but empower them to learn, grow, and create without barriers.

Our goal is clear: protect our systems, improve our applications, and build tools that serve the community, especially those living with neurodivergence or experiencing unwanted loneliness. At The Backtrack Knights, technology is not only a defense: it is a bridge to unite, support, and give freedom to those who need it most.

We also participate in open-source projects, contributing tools and resources that benefit the broader tech community. Through these activities, we aim not only to protect but also to empower our members and the community as a whole.

How Can I Join?

To join The Backtrack Knights, it is essential that you share our passion for cybersecurity and/or software development (preferably web and/or scripting).

It is also essential to be part of the Pana Gaming community and hold the Member rank.

Once you meet these requirements, you should contact AstraTorres99, the leader of The Backtrack Knights. From there, you will be tested and must demonstrate your skills and knowledge in cybersecurity and/or software development.

It is not necessary to fully master both areas‚Äîcybersecurity and software development. It is recommended but not required. What truly matters is the desire to learn, share, and protect. We value spirit, motivation, and passion for these fields (or one of them) far more than prior experience.

Most importantly, besides a passion for Cybersecurity and/or Software Development, at Pana Gaming and The Backtrack Knights we deeply value personality: we seek people who are ethical, empathetic, respectful, with strong principles, and capable of working from kindness and honesty. We want those who join us to reflect the essence of Pana Gaming: community, integrity, and humanity.
